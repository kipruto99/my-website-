About me

The man behind the screen

Hi, I am a creative and talented individual who holds a Bachelor's degree in Chemistry. However, my passion lies in video editing and graphic design. I have honed my skills in Adobe InDesign, Illustrator and Photoshop and I am now a front end designer and developer.

My experience has allowed me to bring my imagination to life, resulting in unique and visually appealing designs. I bring my knowledge of design and development to every project, ensuring that the end result not only looks great, but functions seamlessly for the user.

Aside from my professional life, I am an avid lover of nature and capturing its beauty through photography. I enjoy watching sunsets and taking pictures, as well as taking leisurely walks in parks. My appreciation for beauty and creativity is evident in my work, and I am always striving to perfect my craft.
